About Me

A little bit about where I've been and how I work.

I'm Alexander Irwin, a software developer and game designer based in Elkton, Maryland, with a B.A. in Game Studies & eSports (minor: Philosophy) from the University of Delaware.

My experience spans game design, hardware/software engineering, content creation, and event production. I bring a strong foundation in Python, Java, C++, and C#, with hands-on experience in Unity, Godot, and GameMaker Studio 2 across macOS, Windows, and Linux.

I'm passionate about using technology to solve problems — whether that's building core game systems, shipping a Raspberry Pi voice assistant, growing a 611K-subscriber YouTube channel through data-driven strategy, or coordinating a university-wide game jam from the ground up.
