Who I Am

About Me

A little bit about where I've been and how I work.

I'm Alexander Irwin, a game developer with a passion for creating worlds that players remember. I approach every project as both a craftsman and a collaborator — writing tight, maintainable code while keeping the bigger picture in focus.

My background spans the full development cycle: from designing the systems players interact with every frame, to the UI flows that frame the experience, to the levels that tell stories without words. I've led small teams through crunches and calm stretches alike.

When I'm not building games, I'm playing them — obsessively studying what makes a moment land, what breaks immersion, and what keeps someone up until 3am hitting "just one more run."
